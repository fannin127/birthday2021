The Pod
Churchstanton, Somerset
  • 3 Nights
  • 31st January - 3rd February
  • Treehouse in the woods

Description

The Pod at Springwater is a unique, handcrafted property set up amongst the trees. It has two bedrooms: one double, with a large window and a view into the trees and the smaller, twin room, with offset bunk beds. The living room has a smart tv on a swing out bracket. There's also a well equipped bathroom with a great shower. Downstairs is accessed through a trapdoor in the floor to the kitchen. Double doors open onto the backyard, which has an outdoor fireplace, pizza oven and bbq.

The space

As well as the accomodation, guests have full access to the field in front of The Pod, with a Willow grove and mowed pathways. Amongst the Willow, there's also a firepit, den and rope swing. The Pod at Springwater is a great place to reconnect with nature and is in easy day-tripping distance for many of Devon, Somerset and Dorset's highlights. You will also have three pygmy goats as neighbours!
